About EduGuard


EduGuard is a groundbreaking mobile application designed to enhance sexual health education and promote safety through innovative technology.

In a society where open discussions about sex education and harassment can be challenging, EduGuard fills a crucial gap by providing a secure, age-appropriate platform for users to access vital information and report sensitive issues.


Our mission is to empower individuals in Sri Lanka by integrating advanced technologies that address both educational and safety needs.


  • Comprehensive Approach to Sexual Well-Being
  • Technology-Driven Solution
  • Culturally Tailored for Sri Lanka
  • Accessible Anytime, Anywhere

Key Features

The four main functions that shape EduGuard's core purpose and capabilities



Sex Education Platform

Delivers age-appropriate content through face recognition technology, ensuring individuals receive the knowledge they need based on their developmental stage.

Read More

Sexual Harassment Reporting

Leverages advanced voice recognition technology for real-time, confidential reporting of sexual harassment, enabling users to seek help safely and promptly.

Read More

Blogs and Questionnaire

Creates a supportive and respectful space for discussions on sexual health, utilizing Natural Language Processing (NLP) to filter out inappropriate content effectively.

Read More

Sexual Health Platform

Employs image processing technology to help users assess symptoms of sexually transmitted diseases (STDs) and offers relevant clinic recommendations if detected.

Read More

Who Benefits from EduGuard


EduGuard caters to a wide and diverse audience in Sri Lanka, including children, teens, adults, married and unmarried individuals, and both educated and non-educated groups.

Specifically designed to offer support to those who may face social stigma or financial barriers in accessing healthcare.

Individuals from all walks of life can access vital information and services in various settings such as schools, workplaces, and public spaces.


  • Broad Audience Inclusion
  • Equitable Access and Affordability
  • Holistic and Dependable Solution
  • Community-Centric Impact
